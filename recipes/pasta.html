Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Authentic Italian pasta carbonara with crispy pancetta, creamy egg sauce, and freshly grated Pecorino Romano cheese.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Romano, grated
  • 50g Parmesan, grated
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt

Directions

  1. Cook pasta in salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup pasta water.
  2. Fry pancetta in a pan until crispy. Remove from heat.
  3. Whisk eggs with grated cheeses and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Combine: Drain pasta, add to pancetta pan. Off heat, mix in egg sauce quickly.
  5. Adjust with reserved pasta water until creamy.
  6. Serve immediately with extra cheese and pepper.
