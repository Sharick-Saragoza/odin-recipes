Classic Lasagne

Description

Layered pasta sheets with rich meat ragù, béchamel sauce, and melted mozzarella. A comforting Italian classic.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagne sheets
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 800g canned tomatoes
  • 2 cups béchamel sauce
  • 200g mozzarella, sliced
  • 50g Parmesan, grated

Directions

  1. Cook meat sauce: Brown beef with onion, add tomatoes, simmer 30 mins.
  2. Prep pasta: Cook sheets until al dente (or use no-boil type).
  3. Layer: Alternate pasta, meat sauce, béchamel, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  4. Top with Parmesan and mozzarella.
  5. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 45 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  6. Rest for 15 minutes before serving.
